# New Partner Inquiry Workflow

**Workflow Name**: `DEI - New Partner Inquiry`  
**Trigger**: Form submission from website "Get Started" form  
**Goal**: Welcome new leads and begin qualification process

---

## Trigger Configuration

**Trigger Type**: Form Submitted  
**Form Name**: `Daily Event Insurance - Partner Inquiry`

### Form Fields to Capture
| Field | GHL Field Name | Type | Required |
|-------|---------------|------|----------|
| Your Name | `contact.name` | Text | Yes |
| Email Address | `contact.email` | Email | Yes |
| Business Name | `contact.company_name` | Text | Yes |
| Business Type | `contact.custom_field.business_type` | Dropdown | Yes |
| Message | `contact.custom_field.inquiry_message` | Textarea | No |

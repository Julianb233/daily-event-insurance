## Policy Revenue Model

### Per Policy: $40

| Party | Share | Amount |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Insurance Carrier | 65% | $26 |
| Daily Event Insurance | 35% | $14 |

### Partner Commission: $14/policy

- 35% revenue share to platform
- Partners receive commission per covered participant
- Estimated 65% opt-in rate from eligible participants

### Revenue Calculator

```
Monthly Revenue = Participants × 0.65 (opt-in) × $14 (commission)
```

**Example Calculations:**

| Vertical | Monthly Volume | Opt-In | Revenue |
|----------|----------------|--------|---------|
| Gym (500 guests) | 500 | 325 | $4,550 |
| Ski Resort (2,500 daily × 120 days) | 300,000 | 195,000 | $2,730,000/season |
| Wellness (150 treatments) | 150 | 98 | $1,365 |
| Fitness Event (1,000 participants) | 1,000 | 650 | $9,100/event |

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Marketing Consent - $5
- Race day registrations
- Marketing list opt-in only
- Lead generation focused
- *Internal use only for Q1-Q2 2026*

### Tier 2: Day Pass Coverage - $40
- Guest passes, day passes
- Single-day event coverage
- Primary tier for all verticals
- **Active for all partners**

### Tier 3: Active Guard Monthly - $100
- Monthly subscription model
- Accident protection + medical rider
- $3-8/month to end user
- *Future: Requires Sure integration*

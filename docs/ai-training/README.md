## Available Training Documents

### 1. Customer Support Agent
**File:** `AI-CUSTOMER-SUPPORT-TRAINING.md`

**Purpose:** Handle customer inquiries, resolve issues, answer questions, provide support for policyholders and partners.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Account and purchase troubleshooting
- Coverage questions and explanations
- Claims process guidance
- Technical issue resolution
- Policy and refund handling
- Partner inquiry routing

**Tone:** Friendly, helpful, professional, empathetic

---

### 2. Sales Agent
**File:** `AI-SALES-AGENT-TRAINING.md`

**Purpose:** Convert interested prospects into partners, educate on the opportunity, overcome objections, guide through the decision to join.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Value proposition communication
- Lead qualification
- Objection handling (12+ common objections)
- Revenue calculations
- Closing techniques
- Follow-up sequences

**Tone:** Enthusiastic but not pushy, consultative, confident

---

### 3. Onboarding Agent
**File:** `AI-ONBOARDING-AGENT-TRAINING.md`

**Purpose:** Guide new partners through complete setup process, from account creation to first sale.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Step-by-step account setup
- Integration method selection (QR/Web/POS)
- Payout configuration
- Materials deployment
- Staff preparation
- Launch confirmation
- Optimization tips

**Tone:** Supportive, patient, encouraging, proactive
